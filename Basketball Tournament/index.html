How It Works

Teams

We have a line-up of elite teams ready to face off in an epic basketball tournament.

Games

Each team in the line-up will play every other team twice, once as the home team and once as the visitor. The home team gets an extra 5 points to start off. If there is a tie, the teams will play an overtime. This score will be added to the previous score. If there is another tie, there will be a second overtime period which will be added to the total score. This will be done until there is no tie.

Results

The results of this elite tournament will be posted immediately. The results will be displayed as a win-loss record next to the team's name. Make sure you check to see where your favorite team ended up.
